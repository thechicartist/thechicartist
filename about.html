I paint romance, memories, and moments that last a lifetime.

Hand-painted watercolor florals and wedding stationery

Hi, I’m Neetika, Watercolor has always been my language. Each bloom, each petal, and every brushstroke is a whisper of the stories I love to tell — weddings, celebrations, and the quiet beauty of everyday life. I believe in creating art that feels alive, romantic, and uniquely personal.

Over the years, I’ve blended my love for florals, delicate calligraphy, and custom stationery to craft pieces that resonate with hearts. Teaching others to explore their own creativity is equally magical — sharing techniques, watching students’ confidence grow, and seeing their joy as their art comes to life.

Every painting I create is a journey of patience, color, and expression. I pour my heart into every detail, whether it’s a wedding invitation, a bouquet painting, or a small floral study. To me, watercolor is not just a medium — it’s a way of celebrating life’s moments with elegance and soul.

If you’d like to bring your wedding vision to life, collaborate on a custom piece, or explore a class, let’s connect and create something beautiful together.
