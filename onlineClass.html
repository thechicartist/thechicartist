Watercolor Floral Bookmark Live Workshop – Sunflower Edition

Join me for a cozy, creative watercolor session where we’ll paint my signature sunflower bookmark set — a design loved for its warmth, softness, and joyful flow.

In this workshop, you’ll learn how to create loose, expressive sunflowers using simple brush movements, gentle color blending, and an intuitive approach to watercolor. We’ll focus on enjoying the process, building confidence, and letting the paint do its magic — no pressure, no perfection required.

By the end of the session, you’ll walk away with a set of hand-painted sunflower bookmarks that feel personal, beautiful, and truly one of a kind — perfect to keep, gift, or treasure between the pages of your favorite book.

This workshop is beginner-friendly and designed to feel calm, inspiring, and deeply satisfying — just you, your brush, and sunflowers in bloom.

✨ No prior watercolor experience needed
✨ Slow, guided, and confidence-building
✨ A creative keepsake you’ll love using again and again
